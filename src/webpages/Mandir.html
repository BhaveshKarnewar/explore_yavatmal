Sacred Mandirs in Yavatmal

Datta Mandir

📍 Datta chowk, Yavatmal

Dedicated to Lord Dattatreya, this temple is situated in the heart of Yavatmal. The serene atmosphere within its premises creates a conducive environment for prayer and meditation.

Visit

Kamleshwar Shiv Mandir

📍 Lohara, Yavatmal

Enshrining the divine presence of prabhu Shiva, this temple holds profound religous significance. The temple's architecture, adorned with carvings and traditional motifs, stands as a testament to the rich cultural heritage of Yavatmal.

Visit

Jagat Mandir

📍 Yavatmal

Jagat Mandir, located in Yavatmal is a renowned temple dedicated to prabhu Krishna, an incarnation of the Hindu god Vishnu. One of the highlights of Jagat Mandir is the celebration of festivals such as Janmashtami, which is observed with great enthusiasm.

Visit

Shree Swami
Samarth Mandir

📍 Yavatmal

Dedicated to Shree Swami Samarth Maharaj, an eminent saint and spiritual master who is believed to be an incarnation of Lord Dattatreya, the temple exudes an aura of tranquility and reverence.

Visit

Gajanan Maharaj Mandir

📍Ganesh Nagar, Yavatmal

The temple dedicated to Gajanan Maharaj in Yavatmal encapsulates the essence of his teachings and the devotion of his followers. His teachings centered around love, compassion, and the path of self-realization, resonating deeply with devotees seeking spiritual guidance.

Visit

Siddhivinayak Mandir

📍 Yavatmal

Beautiful mandir in residencial area near Ranapratap Gate, Yavatmal. It hosts events such as bhajans, discourses, and religious gatherings, fostering a sense of community among the devotees and promoting the rich cultural heritage of the region.

Visit

Ganpati Mandir

📍Naringe Nagar, Yavatmal

Situated in the heart of Naringe Nagar, this temple holds a special place in the hearts of locals and students who flock here seeking blessings and guidance from prabhu Ganesh ji.

Visit

Mahadev Mandir Chausala

📍Chausala, Yavatmal

Situated on the peak of chausala, this shiva mandir is surrounded with lush green nature, tall trees and clouds. You can witness here one of the best sunsets in yavatmal.

Visit

Shani mandir

📍 Yavatmal

Throughout the year, the Shani Mandir witnesses a steady stream of devotees, particularly on Saturdays, which is considered auspicious for worshiping Lord Shani. Special ceremonies and prayers are conducted on these days to honor the deity and seek his divine protection.
