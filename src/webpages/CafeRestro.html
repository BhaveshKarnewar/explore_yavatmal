Cafes in yavatmal

I have never went on a date, so I dont know very much about cafes in yavatmal. But my friends are well experienced in this field & they suggested following places...

Tree House Cafe

4.5/5 | Affordable

Tree house cafe is an Aesthetic cafe that offers delicious food and a pretty ambience. It is near Datte College and tution classes area. It is one of the best cafes in yavatmal.

Try it Out

Cafe Karma

4.4/5 | Affordable

Pizzas, milkshakes & playful desserts served up in a contemporary space with bright decor.

Try it Out

OneBite Cafe

4.8/5 | Expensive

Best place for Pizza, burger and other italian food. Amazing flavors and delicious recipies, Onebite cafe never disappoints!

Try it Out

CT Cakes & Cafe

4.5/5 | Expensive

CT cakes is a cafeteria that serves fast food, South Indian,Italian and Chinese food. This cafe is famous for it’s service, food quality, high maintenance & hygiene. Also, they have beautiful range of cakes and desserts.

Try it Out

Cafe Bella Ciao

4.5/5 | Affordable

yet another cafe in SBI square. Chaat, rolls, kebabs, sandwitches and fries are some of the popular food items in Cafe Bella Ciao.

Try it Out

Foodezzz Cafe

4.3/5 | Affordable

Foodezzz cafe is near the SBI square market. It serves delicious snacks and mocktails. It is crowded by couples most of the times.

Try it Out

Frost & Feather

3.5/5 | Affordable

Frost & Feather is known for providing mouthwater junk food in Tilakwadi, Yavatmal. The food joint offers a number of signature dishes. You can give it a try !

Try it Out

BLISS The Fusion Resto

3.8/5 | Affordable

BLISS The Fusion Resto offers a pretty range of coffee and other drinks as well as fast food. The cafe is on the Church road near the OneBite cafe.

Try it Out

Cafe First Floor

3.9/5 | Affordable

Near the Arni road, it is one of the well known cafes in yavatmal. Cafe First Floor is more suitable for Birthday celebrations. They have special decoration and arrangements for such kind of events.

Try it Out
