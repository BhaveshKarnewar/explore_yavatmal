Fairs, exhibitions, and other cultural events are very frequent in Yavatmal. Also, we used to go at Uninvited Weddings in top Banquet halls in Yavatmal. It is quite fun and you must do it if you are an Engineering student. IYKYK!

Fair (Mela)

Often held annually or periodically in a some areas of yavatmal. It can vary widely in size and scope, from small community events to large-scale exhibitions or trade shows.
